# Doc 6 · Approval / Sign-off — NorthStar Recycling AP/AR

## F6.1 · Scope-freeze statement
The modules, workflows, business rules, integrations, exceptions, exclusions,
and acceptance criteria documented in this pack are approved as the baseline
for the fixed-bid proposal (COM-07).

## F6.2 · What is being approved
The contents of Docs 1–5 as a baseline: two modules (Invoice Intake, Payment
Matching), their spelled-out business rules, the three named integrations, the
two named reports, and the numeric acceptance bars (SCO-01, SCO-02).

## F6.3 · Exclusions acknowledgement
The client acknowledges as out of scope: PST handling and PST filings;
multi-B/S split invoices; FX/multi-currency; payment initiation; vendor
onboarding; ongoing maintenance beyond the 30-day warranty (SCO-04, COM-07).
Nothing is included by silence.

## F6.4 · Internal PSRC review & approval
The PSRC reviews Solution Fit, Scope Completeness, Architecture Soundness,
Risk & Feasibility, Effort & Timeline, Value & ROI, and Client Impact; its
approval authorises proceeding (COM-06).

## F6.5 · Change-control note
Any new requirement, integration, report, rule, exception, data variation, or
changed assumption after approval — including a bank spec that contradicts
RAID-A07 — may affect cost, timeline, and approach, and is handled via written
change control approved before work starts (COM-02).
